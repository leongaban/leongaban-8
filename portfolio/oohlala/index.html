Case study: Creative direction

OohlalaRealy.com was the first project that Chris Sanders(codepopper.com) and I worked on together on. I came up with the design and Chris developed the code.

Marc Anthony Rodriguez approached us with a desperate need to take his realty property business online. The site needed to stand out and be bold and vibrant for his brand and clientele.

Flickr widgets where used to allow clients quick access to his enormous gallery of apartments, lofts and townhouses.
